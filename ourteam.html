Meet Our Team

Sure, we work with outside consultants and experts, but we like to put the spotlight on our people who work with our kids everyday one on one… We believe great work is achieved by a team of awesome individuals working in sync day in, day out. Besides the kids we work with, our team is what we're most passionate about.
We’ve built a team of people that love what they do. We hire selectively, and groom constantly. All of our behavior technicians have a bachelor’s degree or higher and several are enrolled in getting their board certification (BCBA or BCaBA).
We believe in developing and promoting from within. Between 85 to 95% of our non BT roles are filled internally.
