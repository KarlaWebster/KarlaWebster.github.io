Indianapolis Area Team

Sure, we work with outside consultants and experts, but we like to put the spotlight on our people who work with our kids everyday one on one… We believe great work is achieved by a team of awesome individuals working in sync day in, day out. Besides the kids we work with, our team is what we're most passionate about.
We’ve built a team of people that love what they do. We hire selectively, and groom constantly. All of our therapists have a bachelor’s degree or higher and several are enrolled in getting their board certification (BCBA or BCaBA).

About Bierman ABA

Bierman ABA was established in 2006 with a simple focus on providing great therapy for children with autism and on building a unique, awesome and friendly work culture. Since then, it has been truly gratifying to have helped so many children and to have watched their progress with things such as signs, speech and behavioral development. As we’ve grown, we’ve kept a laser sharp focus on our mission and founding principles.
