Measuring Outcomes for Meaningful Impact:

When it comes to providing excellent care for children with autism, several factors contribute to creating a quality program, but none outweigh the importance of outcomes. Providing the best possible care to children with autism can only occur when the focus of treatment is achieving excellent outcomes.
Upon deciding that we wanted to provide the best possible care for children with autism, we defined best in terms of measurable outcomes: daily achievement, high rates of skill acquisition, client “graduation”, and classroom success following treatment. Focusing on outcome-based clinical excellence, our team is dedicated to measurement and constant improvement.
Additionally, we measure outcomes by simply asking our client families, “How are we doing?”. Every quarter, we gage satisfaction by asking a simple question of whether or not our families would recommend our services to a friend or colleague. This feedback helps us to show if our outcomes are of value to the families we serve.
Delivering meaningful outcomes creates a significant improvement on the lives of the children we serve.
